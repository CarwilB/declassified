TINUE THIS RESEARCH UNTIL THE AID PROGRAM CAN BE IMPLEMENTED IS TO EXTEND THE U OF FLORIDA CONTRACT. THEREFORE, WE RECOMMEND THAT INM AMEND AND EXTEND THE U OF FLORIDA CONTRACT (511-107T) FROM FEBRUARY 6, 1980 THROUGH FEBRUARY 5, 1981 (AGRICULTURAL DEVELOP- MENT IN COCA ZONES GRANT PROJECT 104-0-02-02) TO ENABLE THE FOUR TECHNICIANS TO COMPLETE THE WORK IN PROGRESS AND THEIR TWO-YEAR RESIDENT ASSIGNEMENTS.

  1. OUR ESTIMATE OF THE TOTAL COST OF THIS WORK FOR THE PERIOD FEBRUARY 6, 1980 THROUGH FEBRUARY 5, 1981 IS $356,000. THIS WILL COVER ALL COSTS FOR THE TECHNICIANS AND THEIR SUPPORT, IN- CLUDING THE SERVICES OF A SKILLED LOCAL ADMINISTRATIVE ASSISTANT. WITH THE HIRING OF AN ADMINISTRATIVE ASSISTANT, ONE MEMBER OF THE FLORIDA TEAM MAY BE DESIGNATED AS RESEARCH COORDINATOR AND THE NEED TO REPLACE FOR ONE YEAR THE CURRENT “CHIEF OF PARTY” (WHO WILL DEPART IN FEBRUARY WHETHER THE CONTRACT IS RENEWED OR NOT) WILL BE OBVIATED.
