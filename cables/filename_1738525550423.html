Subject: First Lady’s Bolivian Schedule

Following is Mrs. Carter’s La Paz schedule:

Mrs. Carter’s visit to La Paz, Bolivia August 4-7, 1979

Saturday, August 4, 1979

  • 5:30 P.M. U.S. Air Force aircraft arrives El Alto International Airport, La Paz, Bolivia.
    • Open coverage.
    • Closed arrival.
    • Mrs. Carter will be met by Ambassador Ernesto Ruiz Rada, Director General of Ceremonies.
    • Mrs. Carter, escorted by Ambassador Ruiz, proceeds down red carpet and is greeted by:
      • Foreign Minister Jorge Escobari Cusicanqui
      • Mrs. Laura Cardozo de Escobari
      • Mrs. Marina Goytia de Padilla, wife of President
    • Note: Mrs. Carter will be presented with a bouquet of flowers by either Mrs. Padilla or Mrs. Escobari.
    • Mrs. Carter, escorted by Ambassador Ruiz, proceeds further down red carpet and greets:
      • Ambassador Carlos Costa Du Rels, Director of Protocol
      • Colonel Ricardo Sanchez, Mayor of La Paz
      • Ambassador and Mrs. Paul Boeker (Margaret)
      • Deputy Chief of Mission and Mrs. Williams Beal (Robbie)
    • Guest and staff instructions: Proceed to motorcade for boarding. Assignments are as follows:
      • Lead
      • Spare: K. Le Grand
      • Mrs. Carter’s Vehicle: Mrs. Carter
      • USSS Follow-up
      • Control: H. Hoyt, M. Macbean, M. Fackelman
      • Staff: S. Van Reigersberg, K. Dobelle, J. Fenderson
      • Guest and Staff Bus: Amb. Vaky, R. Bonilla, R. Burris, W. Mixson, B. Pastor, D. Barnebey, K. Hays, J. Clark, G. Guerra, M. Crowell, L. Dewan, E. Bjerke, Dr. Falk
      • Tail
  • 5:35 P.M. Mrs. Carter, escorted by the Mayor, proceeds to speakers’ platform and remains standing.
  • 5:36 P.M. Welcoming remarks by either the Foreign Minister or Mrs. Padilla.
  • 5:38 P.M. Remarks by Mayor, concluding in the introduction of Mrs. Carter.
  • 5:40 P.M. Remarks by Mrs. Carter.
    • Open press coverage.
  • 5:45 P.M. Mrs. Carter proceeds to motorcade for boarding.

Sunday, August 5, 1979

  • 5:50 P.M. Motorcade departs El Alto International Airport en route to La Paz Sheraton Hotel.
    • (Driving time: 20 minutes)
  • 6:10 P.M. Motorcade arrives La Paz Sheraton Hotel.
    • Press pool coverage. Closed arrival.
    • Mrs. Carter will be met by:
    • Guest and staff instructions: Proceed to hotel rooms. Room list attached. Keys will be in your doors. Free evening.
  • 6:12 P.M. Mrs. Carter proceeds to suite.
  • 6:15 P.M. Mrs. Carter arrives suite.
    • Free evening.
    • RON.
  • 3:40 P.M. Guest and staff instructions: Proceed to motorcade for boarding. Assignments are as follows:
    • Lead
    • Spare: K. Legrand
    • Mrs. Carter’s Vehicle: Mrs. Carter, Ambassador Vaky, Amb. Boeker
    • Control: M. Hoyt, M. Macbeal, M. Fackelman
    • Staff: R. Bonilla, R. Burris, W. Mixson
    • Tail
  • 3:45 P.M. Mrs. Carter departs suite en route motorcade for boarding.
  • 3:50 P.M. Motorcade departs La Paz Sheraton Hotel en route Government Palace.
    • (Driving time: 10 minutes)
  • 4:00 P.M. Motorcade arrives Government Palace.
    • Press pool coverage. Closed arrival.
    • Mrs. Carter will be greeted by:
      • Mr. Pablo Jimenez, Counselor for Protocol
      • Mr. Antonio Salgado, Second Secretary for Protocol
    • Guest and staff instructions: Official party follow Mrs. Carter.
    • Mrs. Carter, escorted by Mr. Jimenez and Mr. Salgado, proceeds inside Government Palace en route Red Room.
    • Mrs. Carter arrives Red Room and greets other delegations.
  • 4:15 P.M. Mrs. Carter, escorted by Ambassador Costa Du Rels, departs Red Room en route Hall of Mirrors.
  • 4:16 P.M. Mrs. Carter arrives Hall of Mirrors and is presented to President David Padilla Arancibia by Ambassador Costa Du Rels.
  • 4:17 P.M. Mrs. Carter presents her credentials to President Padilla.
  • 4:18 P.M. Mrs. Carter greets President Padilla and Foreign Minister Jorge Escobari.
  • 4:19 P.M. Mrs. Carter presents members of U.S. delegation to President Padilla.
  • 4:20 P.M. Mrs. Carter thanks President Padilla and, escorted by Mr. Jimenez and Mr. Salgado, departs Hall of Mirrors en route motorcade for boarding.
    • Guest and staff instructions: Proceed to motorcade. Assignments as on arrival.
  • 4:25 P.M. Motorcade departs Government Palace en route Ambassador Boeker’s residence.
    • (Driving time: 10 minutes)
  • 4:35 P.M. Motorcade arrives Ambassador’s residence.
    • Mrs. Carter, escorted by Ambassador Boeker, proceeds inside residence en route garden area.
  • 4:40 P.M. Mrs. Carter arrives garden and proceeds to podium to greet American Embassy personnel and members of the American community in La Paz.
  • 4:41 P.M. Remarks by Mrs. Carter.
    • Press pool coverage.
  • 4:46 P.M. Remarks conclude.
  • 4:47 P.M. Mrs. Carter proceeds inside garden and greets American guests.
  • 5:00 P.M. Mrs. Carter thanks her hosts and proceeds inside residence en route holding room.
  • 5:02 P.M. Mrs. Carter arrives holding room.
    • Personal/staff time: 1 hour, 58 minutes.
  • 6:40 P.M. Guest and staff instructions: Proceed to bus for buffet at Ambassador’s residence.
    • Participants: S. Reigersberg, K. Dobelle, J. Fenderson, B. Pastor, D. Barnebey, K. Hays, J. Clark, G. Guerra, M. Crowell, L. Dewan, E. Bjerke.
  • 6:50 P.M. Bus departs La Paz Sheraton en route Ambassador’s residence.
  • 6:57 P.M. Bus arrives Ambassador’s residence.
    • Guest and staff instructions: Proceed inside for buffet.
  • 7:00 P.M. Mrs. Carter departs holding room for informal buffet dinner followed by a musical presentation by two Bolivian folk groups.
  • 8:55 P.M. Guest and staff instructions: Proceed to motorcade for boarding. Assignments as on arrival.
  • 9:00 P.M. Mrs. Carter thanks her hosts and proceeds to motorcade for boarding.
  • 9:05 P.M. Motorcade departs Ambassador’s residence en route La Paz Sheraton Hotel.
    • (Driving time: 7 minutes)

Monday, August 6, 1979

  • 9:12 P.M. Motorcade arrives La Paz Sheraton Hotel.
    • Closed arrival.
    • Mrs. Carter proceeds to suite.
  • 9:18 P.M. Mrs. Carter arrives suite.
    • RON.
  • 9:20 A.M. Guest and staff instructions: Proceed to motorcade for boarding. Assignments are as follows:
    • Lead
    • Spare: K. Legrand
    • Mrs. Carter’s Vehicle: Mrs. Carter, Amb. Boeker, Amb. Vaky
    • USSS Follow-up
    • Control: M. Hoyt, M. Macbean, M. Fackelman
    • Staff: R. Bonilla, R. Burris, W. Mixson
  • 9:25 A.M. Mrs. Carter departs suite en route motorcade for boarding.
  • 9:30 A.M. Motorcade departs La Paz Sheraton Hotel en route to Legislative Palace.
    • (Driving time: 10 minutes)
  • 9:40 A.M. Motorcade arrives Legislative Palace.
    • Press pool coverage. Closed arrival.
    • Mrs. Carter will be greeted by:
      • Mrs. Maritza de Carazas, Second Secretary for Protocol
      • Gladys Costa Du Rels, Mrs. Carter’s Bolivian escort
    • Guest and staff instructions: Official party follow Mrs. Carter to box.
  • 9:42 A.M. Mrs. Carter, escorted by Mrs. de Carazas and Mrs. Costa Du Rels, proceeds inside Legislative Palace en route Presidential Box of the Congressional Chamber.
  • 9:45 A.M. Mrs. Carter arrives Presidential Box and takes her seat for inauguration ceremonies.
    • Press pool coverage. Attendance:
  • 10:00 A.M. Inauguration ceremonies begin.
  • 1:00 P.M. Inauguration ceremonies conclude.
    • Guest and staff instructions: Proceed to motorcade for boarding. Assignments as on arrival.
  • 1:00 P.M. Mrs. Carter departs Presidential Box en route motorcade for boarding.
  • 1:05 P.M. Motorcade departs Legislative Palace en route La Paz Sheraton Hotel.
    • (Driving time: 10 minutes)
  • 1:15 P.M. Motorcade arrives La Paz Sheraton Hotel.
    • Mrs. Carter proceeds to suite.
  • 1:20 P.M. Mrs. Carter arrives suite.
    • (Personal/staff time: 4 hrs. 50 min.)
  • 6:05 P.M. Guest and staff instructions: Proceed to motorcade for boarding. Assignments as in previous motorcade.
  • 6:10 P.M. Mrs. Carter departs suite en route Government Palace.
    • (Driving time: 10 minutes)
  • 6:25 P.M. Motorcade arrives Government Palace.
    • Mrs. Carter will be greeted by:
      • Mr. Pablo Jimenez, Counselor for Protocol
      • Mr. Antonio Salgado, Second Secretary for Protocol
    • Official party instructions: Follow Mrs. Carter.
    • Mrs. Carter, escorted by Mr. Jimenez and Mr. Salgado, proceeds inside Government Palace en route Red Room.
    • Mrs. Carter arrives Red Room and greets other delegations.
  • 6:45 P.M. Mrs. Carter, escorted by Ambassador Costa Du Rels, departs Red Room en route Hall of Mirrors.
  • 6:46 P.M. Mrs. Carter arrives Hall of Mirrors and is presented to the newly elected President of Bolivia by Ambassador Costa Du Rels.
  • 6:47 P.M. Mrs. Carter greets the new President.
  • 6:48 P.M. Mrs. Carter presents members of U.S. delegation to the new President.
  • 6:50 P.M. Mrs. Carter thanks the President and, escorted by Mr. Jimenez and Mr. Salgado, departs Hall of Mirrors en route motorcade for boarding.
    • Guest and staff instructions: Proceed to motorcade. Assignments as on arrival.
  • 6:55 P.M. Motorcade departs Government Palace en route La Paz Sheraton Hotel.
    • (Driving time: 10 minutes)
  • 7:05 P.M. Motorcade arrives La Paz Sheraton Hotel.
    • Mrs. Carter proceeds to suite.
  • 7:10 P.M. Mrs. Carter arrives suite.
    • (Personal/staff time: 2 hrs. 15 min.)
  • 9:30 P.M. Guest and staff instructions: Proceed to motorcade for boarding. Assignments as in previous motorcade.
  • 9:25 P.M. Mrs. Carter departs suite en route motorcade for boarding.
  • 9:30 P.M. Motorcade departs La Paz Sheraton en route to Government Palace.
    • (Driving time: 10 minutes)
  • 9:40 P.M. Motorcade arrives Government Palace.
    • Press pool coverage. Closed arrival.
    • Mrs. Carter will be met by:
      • Ambassador Costa Du Rels
      • Mrs. Carazas
    • Official party instructions: Proceed inside Palace to reception area.
  • 9:42 P.M. Mrs. Carter, escorted by Ambassador Costa Du Rels and Mrs. Carazas, proceeds inside Palace Hall and greets other delegations.
    • Note: At 10:00 P.M. the newly elected President will arrive. The Bolivian National Anthem will be played and following that, the reception begins.
  • 11:15 P.M. Official party instructions: Proceed to motorcade for boarding. Assignments are as on arrival.
  • 11:20 P.M. Mrs. Carter thanks her hosts and proceeds to motorcade for boarding.
  • 11:25 P.M. Motorcade departs Government Palace en route to Sheraton Hotel.
    • (Driving time: 10 minutes)
  • 11:35 P.M. Motorcade arrives La Paz Sheraton Hotel.
    • Mrs. Carter proceeds to suite.
  • 11:40 P.M. Mrs. Carter arrives suite.
    • RON.

Tuesday, August 7, 1979

  • Baggage call: All bags must be placed outside your rooms in an unlocked condition no later than 7:00 A.M.
  • 9:15 A.M. Guest and staff instructions: Proceed to motorcade for boarding. Assignments are as follows:
    • Lead
    • Spare: K. Legrand
    • Mrs. Carter’s Vehicle: Mrs. Carter
    • USSS Follow-up
    • Control: M. Hoyt, M. Macbean, M. Fackelman
    • Staff: S. Van Reigersberg, K. Dobelle, J. Fenderson
    • Guest and Staff Bus: Amb. Vaky, B. Pastor, D. Barnebey, K. Hays, J. Clark, G. Guerra, M. Crowell, L. Dewan, E. Bjerke
  • 9:20 A.M. Mrs. Carter departs suite en route motorcade for boarding.
  • 9:25 A.M. Motorcade departs La Paz Sheraton Hotel en route to El Alto International Airport.
    • (Driving time: 20 minutes)
  • 9:45 A.M. Motorcade arrives El Alto International Airport.
    • Open press coverage. Closed departure.
    • Mrs. Carter is greeted by:
      • Colonel Ricardo Sanchez, Mayor of La Paz
    • Guest and staff instructions: You will be escorted to viewing area.
  • 9:47 A.M. Mrs. Carter, escorted by the Mayor, proceeds to speakers’ platform.
  • 9:47 A.M. Remarks by the Mayor.
  • 9:50 A.M. Remarks conclude.
  • 9:51 A.M. Remarks by Mrs. Carter.
  • 9:55 A.M. Remarks conclude.
    • Guest and staff instructions: Board aircraft. Manifest as on arrival, except delete:
      • R. Bonilla
      • R. Burris
      • W. Mixson
      • Dr. Falk
    • Mrs. Carter, escorted by the Mayor, bids farewell to:
      • Ambassador Ernesto Ruiz Rada, Director General of Ceremonies
      • Foreign Minister Jorge Escobari Cusicanqui
      • Mrs. Laura Cardozo de Escobari
      • Mrs. Marina Goytia de Padilla
      • Ambassador Carlos Costa Du Rels, Director of Protocol
      • Colonel Ricardo Sanchez, Mayor of La Paz
      • Ambassador and Mrs. Paul Boeker (Margaret)
      • Deputy Chief of Mission and Mrs. Beal (Robbie)
  • 9:58 A.M. Mrs. Carter boards aircraft.
  • 10:00 A.M. U.S. Air Force aircraft departs La Paz, Bolivia, en route Cotopaxi Air Base, Ecuador.
